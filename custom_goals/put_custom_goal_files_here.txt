Drop a .md file in this directory for each custom practice goal you want to
choose from at the start of a session (see practice_modes.py). For example:

    custom_goals/restaurant_vocab.md
    custom_goals/job_interview.md

The filename (underscores turned into spaces) becomes that goal's menu label,
and the file's full text is given to the tutor as the session's goal, so just
describe what you want to focus on in plain language, e.g.:

    Practice ordering food at a restaurant: greeting the waiter, asking about
    the menu, ordering, and asking for the bill.

You can reference the current session's languages with {NATIVE_LANGUAGE} and
{TARGET_LANGUAGE} -- they're substituted with whatever you picked at startup
(e.g. English / German), so a goal file can stay language-agnostic:

    Quiz me on restaurant vocabulary: ask the word in {NATIVE_LANGUAGE} and
    have me answer in {TARGET_LANGUAGE}.

When you pick "Custom goal" at the start of a session, LangTeacher lists the
5 most recently modified .md files here for you to choose from by number.

This directory is gitignored (see .gitignore) so pulling an app update never
touches your own goal files.
